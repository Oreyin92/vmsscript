Creche

At VMS one of our core value is child-centeredness, to fulfil this to the core wehave added creche to our list of educational services. Our creche is equipped with modern facilities that will caters to the needs of babies and toddlers, giving parents the required peace of mind they need to perform brillinatly at their place of work

If you are looking for a home far away from home, then Virtue Model school is the place for your child. Our creche cares for babies from ages 3 to 18 months in a cozy enviroment.

We have the best Creche!!!

We believes in providing a well-rounded education for children; and we aim to build a solid foundation for our pupils and students.
While you parents are being productive during the day, we are here giving your child the best care him or her deserves, our creche is truly design to give the best care fr your babies.

Why Choose Our Creche

Listed below are some of the benefits you get when enrol your child with us:

  • Passionate Caregivers

Babies and toddlers are very fragile and vulnerable as they are at the early stage of development we understand this perfectly at VMS, and the main reason why we only employ trained and qualified caregivers who have the natural affinity for toddlers.

  • Big, Well-Equipped Rooms & Dedicated Caregivers

At VMS we provide the best setting for our creche, the room is spacious with adequate light and ventilation. Our primary objective is to ensure babies in our care are comfortable at all times, babies wet diapers are always changed on time, we ensure they are giving nutritious food according to their need and also, we encourage good habits by practicing through routine activities such as potty training.

  • Play-based Learning

Although it is seen in many creches in Lagos, teaching ABC or 123 rhymes to toddlers and asking them to repeat is quite odd. This is because it has been observed that teaching toddlers through instruction isn’t effective in making them comprehend the basis of what is being taught. For this reason, in our creche, we adopt play-based learning – an integral part of the Montessori teaching method, which is proven to help kids learn faster and comprehend the basis of what they are being taught.
Through play-based learning, your child gets to play, have fun, and still learn the Alphabet and Numbers. This method not only helps the child with speech but enhances the essential skill of communication.

  • Security

We have policies that ensure the safety of your child 100%. Children are only allowed to be picked up by the parents or any registered members of a family. VMS facility is located in a safe and secure location with 24/7 security on guard with trained security personnel that ensures that your creche is protected from all invaders.
Virtue Model School has all it takes to provide quality care and early childhood education for your Precious.
